Pseudocode for Scores
 STAGE 1
Enter numbers 0 to 10
create a table that displays:
the squares of the numbers
the cubes of the numbers
save result 
display result


 STAGE 2

prompt the user to enter 0 to 10 numbers
collect numbers
save as numbers
create a table
input the numbers
calculate the squares of numbers as:
 numbers * numbers
collect result
save as squares of numbers
calculate the cubes of numbers as:
 numbers*numbers*numbers
collect result
save as cubes of numbers
display result
